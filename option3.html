Our Story

At Hareg, we bring the authentic flavors of Ethiopia to your table. Our restaurant is named after the Ethiopian word for "my country," reflecting our deep connection to our homeland and its culinary traditions.

Every dish is prepared using traditional methods and family recipes that have been passed down through generations. We source the finest spices and ingredients to ensure an authentic dining experience.

From our signature Doro Wot to our freshly made Injera, each meal is crafted with care and served with the warm hospitality that Ethiopia is known for. Come join us and experience the rich cultural heritage of Ethiopian cuisine.
